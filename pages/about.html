About

The Sixth City Dynasty Football League was founded in 2021 as a branch off of a standard redraft league with origins in the Sigma Phi Epsilon Ohio Zeta chapter. Rooted in Cleveland, Sixth City is host to members throughout the state of Ohio.

Sixth City celebrates continuity and creativity. Franchises carry their own legacies from year to year, and they carry the glory and ignominy that comes with those legacies. Members concoct experimental formats like 2v2 Tag-Team and One-vs-Everyone matchups. History is preserved and celebrated with the advent of Throwback Week and the forthcoming SCDFL Hall of Fame.

What began as a group of fraternity buddies coming together to enjoy the game of football has grown into a vibrant sports community, contributing to a league that has become an entire universe rich with history, rivalries, and traditions.
